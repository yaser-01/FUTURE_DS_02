# Customer Retention & Churn Analysis 📊

Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing customer data to identify patterns of "churn" (customers leaving) and developing strategies for better retention. The goal is to provide a business with data-driven insights into customer loyalty and lifetime value.

Project Structure
Following a professional analytics workflow, the project is organized as follows:
- **[Data](https://github.com/yaser-01/FUTURE_DS_02/tree/main/data):** Raw customer datasets and cleaning logs.
- **[Reports & Dashboard](https://github.com/yaser-01/FUTURE_DS_02/tree/main/reports/dashboard):** Final analysis files and interactive Excel workbooks.
- **[Visuals](https://github.com/yaser-01/FUTURE_DS_02/tree/main/reports/dashboard/images):** High-resolution screenshots of the analysis dashboard.

Tools Used
* **Microsoft Excel:** Data cleaning, Pivot Tables, and Dashboard creation.
* **KPI Analysis:** Calculating Churn Rate, Retention Rate, and Customer Lifetime Value (CLV).

Key Insights & KPIs
* **Churn Rate:** Identified the percentage of customers stopping service over a specific period.
* **High-Risk Segments:** Discovered which customer demographics or regions have the highest turnover.
* **Retention Drivers:** Analyzed which factors (discounts, contract length, or support quality) most influence customer loyalty.

Actionable Recommendations
1. **Targeted Re-engagement:** Implement automated email campaigns for segments showing early signs of churn.
2. **Loyalty Incentives:** Offer specific rewards to "High-Value" customers identified in the top 20% of revenue.
3. **Service Improvement:** Focus customer support resources on the specific regions showing declining retention rates.
